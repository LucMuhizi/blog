Nite Tanzarn is an intellectual infrastructure advancing **Agency, Authority, and Architecture** to dismantle structural inequality and design systems for equity.

We work at the intersection of research, policy transformation, institutional retrofit, and governance innovation. Our work is grounded in rigour and oriented toward futures that look very different from the present — because the present, for too many people, is not working.

## What we do

- **Research** — original empirical work, field reports, and quantitative analysis grounded in structural inquiry.
- **Policy** — briefs, frameworks, and recommendations for governments, multilaterals, and civil society.
- **Training & convening** — capacity-building for institutions, coalitions, and emerging leaders.
- **Consultancy** — independent engagements with organisations committed to structural change.

## Who we work with

We work with governments, multilaterals, philanthropic foundations, civil society organisations, research institutions, and movements for justice. We do not work with actors whose stated mission conflicts with structural equity.

## Where we are

Nite Tanzarn is based in Mbuya, Kampala, Uganda. Our work travels across continents and time zones; our roots are local.

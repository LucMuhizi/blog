**Annotations** will host close, structured readings of the texts that shape our research and thinking — disciplinary canons, policy documents, field reports, and contemporary interventions worth engaging seriously.

Annotations are not reviews. They are not summaries. They are reading-room notes: confirmed claims, contested claims, and the questions that open up when a text is read slowly.

## Status

Annotations is a section in development. It will open alongside a forthcoming body of work; the first annotated readings will appear in 2026.

## Want to suggest a text?

If there is a text you would like to see annotated — a policy brief, a foundational paper, a primary source — please get in touch.

We offer independent consultancy in research, policy transformation, training, and institutional retrofit. Each engagement is grounded in rigour, anchored in evidence, and oriented toward structural change.

## Engagements

- **Scoping and strategy** — diagnosing the structural conditions that produce a problem, before designing responses.
- **Research and analysis** — empirical work to inform decision-making, including field reports, reviews, and quantitative analysis.
- **Policy design and review** — drafting, redrafting, and reviewing briefs, frameworks, and recommendations.
- **Institutional retrofit** — adapting the architecture of organisations so they can carry new mandates.
- **Training** — building capacity in research methods, structural analysis, gender-responsive policy, and institutional design.
- **Convening** — designing and facilitating gatherings that move from talk to alignment.

## Engagement model

Most engagements begin with a scoping call to clarify the problem, the audience, the timeline, and the budget. From there we propose a work plan, a deliverables schedule, and a fee structure. We do not work on speculative retainers and we do not invoice without a written scope.

## Who we work with

We work with governments, multilaterals, philanthropic foundations, civil society organisations, research institutions, and movements for justice. We do not work with actors whose stated mission conflicts with structural equity.

## How engagements start

The fastest path is a short email describing what you want to achieve, the timeline, and the institutional context. From there we respond with questions and propose the next step.
